10Pearls

Project Date 02/2013 - 07/2014
Role ETL & Data Warehouse Specialist
Client 10Pearls
Visit https://10pearls.com/
Project Description

Constructed ETL workflows using Informatica and SSIS to process more than 500M records daily across retail and telecom data warehouses, while also building Epic Clarity and Caboodle healthcare integrations. Designed and implemented dimensional models such as Star Schema and Data Vault to support BI and analytics workloads. Improved query performance by 35% through model optimization and indexing strategies. Built early prototypes of data contracts that improved upstream and downstream dependency management by 30%. Streamlined batch job scheduling processes and eliminated 70% of manual operations in telecom billing. Tuned POS reporting queries and reduced runtime from 8 hours to just 1 hour. Secured all data pipelines to meet HIPAA, GDPR, and CCPA compliance requirements through IAM, encryption, and data masking, and standardized healthcare ETL pipelines using ICD-10, CPT, LOINC, and SNOMED coding frameworks. Transitioned BI workloads from SSRS to Tableau, increasing analyst adoption by 45%. Designed KPI dashboards for retail forecasting and churn prediction to provide actionable executive insights.
